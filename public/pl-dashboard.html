📈 Dashboard Guide

Layout

Standard consolidated income statement — Revenue down to Net Income. All line items flow as: Revenue → COGS → Gross Profit → OpEx → EBITDA → D&A → EBIT → Interest → EBT → Tax → Net Income.

Annual View

5 fiscal years (FY2020–FY2024) as columns. FY2024 also shows a Budget column and Variance%. Click the + icon on any year header to drill into its Q1–Q4 — the quarterly columns appear immediately next to that year.

Quarterly View

Select any year to show Q1–Q4 plus Full Year and Budget columns.

Filters

Business Unit, Region, and Channel filters re-aggregate all P&L lines dynamically. Export CSV captures the current filtered view.

All figures are simulated for portfolio purposes only.
