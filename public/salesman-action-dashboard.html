    ⓘ Info

    ⚔ Battles

    Customers where the model predicts purchases (Confidence > 0) but who are not yet loyal — they have lower historical engagement. These are opportunities the salesman must “fight” for: visit regularly, push orders, introduce new SKUs, and grow wallet share.

    ⚗ Hold Position

    Customers who are already loyal (high historical activity, frequent purchases). The salesman's job is to maintain the relationship, protect existing orders, fulfil expectations, and prevent churn.
